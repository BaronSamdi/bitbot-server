It's Oh, So Simple!

Designed to be Easy

Bitrule connects you your exchange accounts using custom exchange API's and does not hold or have direct access to your bitcoins or hard currency. As an added layer of security, Bitrule does not supply a method for withdrawing funds from your exchange accounts. This we leave to you. With Bitrule, you can define and place auto-trade rules in any of the major bitcoin exchanges. With just a few intuitive clicks you can place Stop-Loss, Limit Buy\Sell and other orders. Mitigate your risk in case of market price flctuations and maximize profits from price spikes. You can rest easy knowing that even when you are away from the market, Bitrule will activate your trade triggers according to your definitions.
