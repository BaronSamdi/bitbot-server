Welcome!

Bitrule is a cross-market paltform

Place automatic trade rules such as Stop-Loss, Stop-Limit and more in any of the major Bitcoin exchanges, MtGox, Bitstamp, BTCChina and others. With no application or plugin installation needed, it's as simple as A, B, C. Learn more about how it works.

Bitrule is a secure service

Bitrule does not require you to submit any of your personal bitcoin exchange acount information. Instead Bitrule uses custom API's to place your rules in any of your bitcoin exchange accounts.

Learn More »

Bitrule over Google Cloud

Bitrule runs over Google Cloud and provides the most reliable service in the market. Even if you loose your network connectivity or suffer power loss or a computer failure, Bitrule will be up and running, the same way your email provider is always there for you.
